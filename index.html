About us

Empowering safe and smart digital citizens.

At NetGuardians, we’re committed to addressing one of the most pressing challenges in today’s digital world: helping young people and their families become responsible, informed digital citizens.

As an ed-tech company, we work closely with schools, parents, and students to deliver age-appropriate, research-based workshops that educate and empower. Our training covers vital topics such as cybersecurity, cyberbullying, and mental health—filling the critical knowledge gap that often exists between children and the fast-moving digital spaces they inhabit.

Our workshops are designed not just to inform, but to transform. We create safe, engaging learning environments where children and parents can gain real-world tools to navigate online risks confidently and respectfully. We also believe that every child—regardless of background—should have access to this essential education. That’s why we offer discounted programs and pursue endorsements to reach underserved communities.

Driven by a passionate team of educators, technologists, and advocates, our mission is simple:

To equip the next generation with the skills, awareness, and mindset they need to thrive and stay safe in a digital world.

Together, we’re building a future where every young person is prepared to make smart, respectful choices online—and every parent feels empowered to guide them along the way.

Mission Statement

At NetGuardians, our mission is to empower children, parents, and schools with the knowledge and tools needed to navigate the digital world safely and responsibly.


We are committed to bridging the digital literacy gap by delivering impactful, age-appropriate workshops on cybersecurity, cyberbullying, and mental health—ensuring every young person becomes a confident and respectful digital citizen, regardless of their background.

Our Services

What We Do

Collaborate with Schools

We get in touch with schools and explain the breakdown of our program, assess the school needs and agree on terms. We then coordinate dates and times to roll out the workshops.

Read more

Deliver Workshops

We first have a parents session where we present a summary of the program and why it is important for them to be part of the process, as well as equipping them with tools to meet the inevitable questions as well as to better monitor their children's online activity.

We then roll out 4 separate interactive student sessions (1hour each), presented over 4 terms (or three if the school prefers). Each of these sessions will cover 2 topics from the list below.

  • Lesson 1: Digital Footprint
  • Lesson 2: Critical Thinking Online
  • Lesson 3: Privacy Settings
  • Lesson 4: Cybersecurity
  • Lesson 5: Cyberbullying
  • Lesson 6: Mental Health
  • Lesson 7: Sexuality Online
  • Lesson 8: Digital Potential

Coordinate Company CSI’s

Partner under privileged schools with Organizations who seek to make a difference in the lives of children through online behaviour education. In the program, we are able to provide free workshops to deserving schools. NetGuardians also provides discounts to deserving schools.
